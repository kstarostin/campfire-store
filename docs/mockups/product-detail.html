On the road

Fast, composed, and ready for long days on mixed terrain — the Grail CF SL 7 is Canyon’s carbon gravel benchmark with precise handling, a stable ride, and dependable Shimano 105 shifting. DT Swiss aluminium wheels keep it tough without feeling sluggish when the pavement turns to chip seal.

The distinctive Grail frame soaks up chatter without isolating you from the road, so you stay fresh on century rides and rough backroads alike. For Campfire’s demo catalog, this is the gravel bike we’d point you at when you want one bike for training loops, light bikepacking, and the occasional fondo.
